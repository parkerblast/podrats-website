What are the Elephalgos?

Elephalgos are a collection of 1,000 uniquely generated NFTs that accompany a 2.5D platformer as a weekly competition for holders to earn unique NFT prizes. This project also aims to give back 10% of sales to elephant conservation through the African Wildlife Foundation!
